About Rahul Industries

Precision Manufacturing Solutions – Built to Grow With You

Founded in 2012, Rahul Industries is a precision engineering and toolroom company specializing in the manufacturing of high-quality press tools, dies & moulds, jigs, fixtures, and custom precision components.

With over 14+ years of industry experience, we have evolved from a small workshop into a trusted industrial manufacturing partner. Our advanced machining capabilities and skilled engineers allow us to deliver reliable solutions for industries that demand absolute accuracy and performance.

  • High-Precision CNC & VMC Machining
  • Manufacturing of Press Tools, Dies & Moulds
  • Custom Jigs, Fixtures & Inspection Gauges
  • Rapid Prototyping & Contract Manufacturing
  • Trusted by Automotive, Medical & Industrial Sectors
