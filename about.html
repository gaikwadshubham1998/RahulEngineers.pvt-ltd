Our Journey

Founded in 2012, Rahul Industries began as a small precision toolroom with a clear vision to provide high-accuracy manufacturing solutions. Over the last 14+ years, we have grown into a reliable precision engineering partner for industries that demand strict tolerances and high-performance tooling.

Our facility integrates modern machining technologies including CNC machining, VMC milling, precision grinding and conventional turning operations. These capabilities allow us to manufacture high-precision press tools, dies & moulds, jigs, fixtures, inspection gauges and custom engineered components.

Today Rahul Industries supports multiple sectors including automotive, pump & valve manufacturing, medical devices, defense and industrial machinery. We continuously upgrade our technology and strengthen our skilled workforce to deliver reliable and scalable manufacturing solutions.
