Our Journey

Established in 1998, RAHUL ENGINEERS has grown from a small consultancy firm to a full-scale engineering powerhouse. Over the decades, we have successfully executed over 200 projects across various sectors including automative, civil infrastructure, and heavy machinery.

Our success is driven by our people. We believe in nurturing talent and fostering a culture of continuous learning and improvement. This approach has allowed us to stay ahead of the curve and adopt the latest technologies in our operations.
